General Principles for "Lively and Fun" (while maintaining professionalism):Micro-interactions: Subtle hover effects on buttons (slight scale/darken) and interactive cards (slight lift/shadow change).Loading States: Use simple, custom animations (e.g., pulsing Sophera logo using Primary Brand Color, or a gentle wave animation with Teal and Coral) or messages like "Gathering insights for you..."Empty States: Use a relevant Material Symbol icon (colored with Primary Brand or Secondary Accent, size 48px) and encouraging text (H3 style) with a clear Primary or Secondary Accent Button CTA. Example: For "My Plan": Seedling icon, "Let's plant the seeds for your plan!", "+ Add First Item" (Secondary Accent Button).Positive Reinforcement: Brief, positive toast notifications (Background: Primary Brand Color (#0D9488), Text: White (#FFFFFF), with a success icon) for actions like "Journal entry saved!" or "Plan item updated!".Strategic Color Use: Use Secondary Accent Color (#FF7F50) for positive CTAs, icons in "Hope Snippet" or "Connect & Hope", and as one of the colors in charts. Use Tertiary Accent (#FFC107) for other highlights or uplifting icons.This revised specification provides more concrete details for colors, backgrounds (including the gradient), typography, and component styling to achieve the desired "lively and fun" yet professional and supportive aesthetic for Sophera.
