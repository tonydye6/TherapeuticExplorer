│
├── **Authentication**
│   ├── Login Page (Patient / Caregiver)

    │   │   └── Journal History View (List/Calendar)
    │   ├── **Diet Log**
    │   │   ├── Diet Log Input Form
    │   │   └── Diet Log History View

    │

    │   ├── Survivor Stories (Library/View)
    │   ├── Mindfulness Corner (Resource Links)
